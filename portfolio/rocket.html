The Rocket Flyer

A Wagon Concept For Young Speedfreaks
Designed Fall 2012 for DSGN 307: Intro to Industrial Design Methods

The Rocket Flyer was born from fond childhood memories of long afternoons in the cul-de-sac with the neighboring kids. My Radio Flyer was the trusty steed of my youth, before bikes and sailboats and fast cars eventually took their turn. But why was my trusty steed built like a pickup truck when sometimes I needed a drag racer?

This is the all-purpose wagon I dreamed about but grew up too quickly to find.
This is the Rocket Flyer.

The following is a piece of the design process: sketches, drawings, and Solidworks models to translate that dream into something a bit more visible.
